✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Seedance 2.0 free to use? +
Yes! After signing up and completing the daily check-in, you receive free credits to generate videos. No credit card required to get started.
What's the difference between Seedance 1.0 and 2.0? +
Seedance 2.0 is a major leap forward — it provides up to 2K resolution (vs 1080p in 1.0), supports longer video lengths, produces significantly more photorealistic output, and introduces native audio-visual synchronization and multi-reference input support.
How does Seedance 2.0 compare to Sora 2? +
Seedance 2.0 excels at quick, high-quality, stylized video clips with fast generation times and strong character consistency. Sora 2 targets detailed, long-form cinematic storytelling. For fast creative iterations and social-first content, Seedance 2.0 has a clear edge.
What types of inputs does Seedance 2.0 support? +
Seedance 2.0 accepts text prompts, reference images, video clips, and audio files. You can combine up to 12 inputs in a single generation for maximum creative control.
How long does generation take? +
Most videos are generated in under 10 seconds. Longer or higher-resolution outputs may take slightly longer, but you'll rarely wait more than 30 seconds.
Can I use generated videos commercially? +
Commercial usage rights depend on your subscription plan. Free-tier videos are for personal and non-commercial use. Pro plans include full commercial licensing.
